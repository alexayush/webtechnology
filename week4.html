FOOTBALL MANAGER

Football Manager is a soccer management simulation game that allows players to take on the role of a team manager and guide their team to success. In the game, players are responsible for managing all aspects of their team, including tactics, training, team selection, and transfer negotiations. The game features a vast database of players and teams, with realistic statistics and attributes for each player. This allows players to make informed decisions about their team and strategy.
